India’s Chandrayaan-3 Successfully Lands on Moon’s South Pole

By Kavya Rao | August 23, 2023

India’s Chandrayaan-3 mission achieved a historic milestone by becoming the first spacecraft to successfully land on the Moon’s south polar region. The Vikram lander touched down flawlessly, and the Pragyan rover began exploring the lunar surface, collecting valuable data on water ice presence. This success positions India as a key player in future lunar exploration efforts and potential lunar resource utilization. Scientists are analyzing the data to understand the Moon's composition, which could support future human missions.

The image, a haunting black-and-white portrait, fooled expert judges who had no idea it wasn’t shot with a camera. Man Runs Entire Marathon in Jeans and Crocs, Sets World Record

By Laura Chen | October 2023

Michael Goldstein, a 34-year-old runner from the U.S., completed the Chicago Marathon wearing denim jeans and classic Crocs — and not only finished, but set an unofficial world record for the fastest marathon run in that unusual attire. Clocking in at 3 hours 48 minutes, Goldstein said he wanted to prove comfort is subjective. Spectators cheered as he crossed the finish line, and photos of his outfit quickly went viral. Experts say running a full marathon in Crocs could increase risk of injury, but Goldstein reported no pain and declared himself “ready for next year.”
